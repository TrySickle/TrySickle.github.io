LMC 3308 Final Project

Created for Environmentalism and Ecocriticism Spring 2018 at Georgia Tech. This is a visual exhibit of artifacts collected over the course of the semester that all share a common theme of portraying nature in a political way. The network of connections shows their similarities in production, themes, and uses of nature. The goal of the project is to highlight the many similarities between different media forms.

Hover your mouse over each image to see the full metadata and description. Mouse over a connection to see analysis on the link.
